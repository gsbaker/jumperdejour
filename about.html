Hi. We're George and Mark, the founders of Jumper de Jour.

We started this company because, well, we love jumpers.

But, we found it difficult to find cashmere jumpers that were a bit more fun and interesting.

So, we decided to make our own.

Currently, Jumper de Jour is just a vision. A fun little side-project that might turn into something more serious one day.

But if you like the idea of fun, interesting cashmere jumpers, please get in touch and register your interest on the home page.
